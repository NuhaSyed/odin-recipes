Protein Smoothie

Description

Looking for a tasty after workout smoothie? Look no further. This is my go to after a hard gym session.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen cherries
  • 1 scoop Optimal Nutrition double chocolate protein powder
  • 3/4 cup milk

Steps

  1. Peel a banana and place it in your blender
  2. Add frozen cherries, milk, and protein powder
  3. Blend until fully combined
  4. Enjoy!
