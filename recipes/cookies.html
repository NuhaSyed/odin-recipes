Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Ever since I was the size of a wee little chocolate chip myself, I have loved chocolate chip cookies. After a lifetime of experimentation, this is the best and only chocolate chip recipe you will ever need!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Chocolate chips
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Joy and whimsy
  • The blessing of an ancient god
  • Coconut flakes

Steps

  1. Mix the wet ingredients in one bowl
  2. Mix the dry ingredints in another bowl, saving the coconut flakes for later
  3. Mix the dry ingredients into the wet in small portions, mixing to combine
  4. Shape the combined dough into tablespoon sized balls on a lined baking tray
  5. Sprinkle each cookie with coconut flakes
  6. Place the cookies in the oven to bake
  7. Dance in your kitchen to use up any remaining joy and whimsy
